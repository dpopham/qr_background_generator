Are you tired of all those snazzy video conference call background images?

Do you want to let your inner geek express itself using a background image displaying your very own QR code?

Simply fill in this form with a prefered text message or a destination URL and a 1280x720 white image background called "background.png" will be generated with the embedded QR code in the uper left. Set this image as your background in your favorite video conferencing tool and hopefully you will appear in the center with your QR code visible over your shoulder.
